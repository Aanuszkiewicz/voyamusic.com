About Me

Hi, I’m Voy Anuszkiewicz. I’ve spent over 30 years creating unforgettable events, beginning with my early experience performing in my father’s live band, Rytmical. Those formative years shaped my approach: combining technical expertise with musical intuition and genuine passion.

I’ve had the privilege of performing at weddings across New England, New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Florida, Wisconsin, and Mexico. My specialty is blending Polish tradition with modern American weddings. That's what I call simple elegance.

In recent years, I’ve focused primarily on MCing weddings alongside some of New England’s top DJs. When you book Voy, you’re getting more than a DJ - you’re getting an experienced MC, musician, vocalist, sound engineer, and wedding consultant, all at a straightforward wedding DJ price. Free consultations are always available.
